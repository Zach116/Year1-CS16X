When entering the command line, enter the executable name, a space, and then the size of board.
Board size options:
8 (for 8X8)
10 (for 10X10)
12 (for 12X12)

Ex: a.out 8

How To Move:
First, choose a piece to move when prompted
Next, enter the path without including the position of the piece you chose

Ex:
Choose Piece: A6
Path Option 1: B5
Path Option 2: C4 A2
